Bread

Back to Odin Recipes

Description

This easy bread recipe takes very little time to make, and it fills the house with the best smell while it's baking! Makes one large loaf.

Recipe

Ingredients

  • 5 - 6 cups of bread flour
  • 2.5 - 3 cups warm water (not hot)
  • 3 tsp salt
  • 1 Tbsp instant yeast

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, or the bowl of a stand mixer, mix 5 cups of the flour, the salt, and the yeast.
  2. Mix in a scant 3 cups of warm water. Stir until everything is thoroughly moistened. The mixture will be gloopy.
  3. Mix vigorously (by hand or with the mixer) for 5 minutes, or until the gloop starts to become stringy.
  4. Add in more flour until the dough can hold its shape. It will still be a very soft, wet dough.
  5. Cover and let rise for 2-4 hours. The dough will increase in volume and become bubbly.
  6. Turn the dough out onto a well-floured surface. Gently shape the dough into a circle. Transfer the dough to a floured baking sheet or dutch oven.
  7. Cover gently with a clean towel and let the dough rise again for an hour while you preheat the oven to 450F.
  8. Bake the loaf for 60 minutes on a baking sheet. Or bake in a covered dutch oven for 45 minutes, then bake uncovered for 15-20 minutes.
  9. For best results, cool completely before slicing, but it's irresistable warm!

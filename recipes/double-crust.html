Double Crust

Description

This is an easy and delicious pie crust. Makes a top and bottom crust.

Recipe

Ingredients

  • all purpose flour
  • cubed butter
  • cubed crisco
  • Salt
  • Ice water

Steps

  1. Weigh and mix flour and salt.
  2. Add cubed chilled butter and crisco. Chop into the flour until the fat is in pea-sized clumps.
  3. Pour in a small amount of ice water. Gently mix until the dough just holds together.
  4. Dump the crust onto a board and press together just until it holds its shape.
  5. Divide the dough in two parts, flatten each half into a disk, wrap in plastic wrap and refrigerate for an hour or up to a few days.
  6. When you're ready to make the pie, preheat the oven according to your recipe. Roll out each half about 1/8" thick. Lay in the pie plate. Blind bake or not, depending on the recipe. Roll out and add the top crust the same way.
  7. Bake the crust according to the recipe, or until golden brown and bubbly.
